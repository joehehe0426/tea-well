Our Story

Located in the heart of Cheung Sha Wan, 茶井奶蓋專門店 Tea Well is your go-to destination for premium cheese milk foam tea. We specialize in 王牌芝士奶蓋 — our award-winning cheese milk foam that you can refill unlimited times.

From classic pearl milk tea to refreshing fruit teas, every drink is made fresh to order with quality ingredients. Come taste the difference that made us Cheung Sha Wan's favorite bubble tea spot.
