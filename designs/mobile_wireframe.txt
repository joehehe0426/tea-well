Mobile Wireframe for Tea Wellness Website

Header:
- Logo (left) | Search icon (center) | Menu/hamburger icon (right)

Hero Section:
- Full-width background image of tea leaves/tea cup
- Overlay text: "Discover Natural Wellness"
- Subtext: "Premium teas for mind and body"
- Primary button: "Shop Our Collection"
- Secondary button: "Learn About Benefits"

Features Section:
- 3-column stacked layout (mobile)
- Each column: Icon + short text (e.g., "Organic", "Sustainably Sourced", "Health Benefits")

Product Highlights:
- Horizontal scrollable carousel (or vertical stack) of featured teas
- Each tea card: Image, name, short description, price, "Add to Cart" button

About Section:
- Brief brand story with image on one side, text on the other (stacked on mobile)
- Call-to-action: "Our Story"

Testimonials:
- Vertical stack of testimonial cards
- Each card: quote, customer name, photo

Newsletter:
- Input field for email
- Submit button: "Subscribe for 10% Off"

Footer:
- Column 1: Logo + brief description
- Column 2: Quick Links (About, Shop, Blog, Contact)
- Column 3: Social Media Icons
- Column 4: Newsletter signup (mini)
- Bottom: Copyright and legal links

Design Notes:
- Color palette: Earthy greens, warm beiges, soft whites
- Typography: Clean sans-serif for body, elegant serif for headings
- Imagery: High-quality photos of tea plantations, tea cups, natural ingredients
- Spacing: Generous padding and white space for calm, inviting feel
